The Most Popular Song on my Birthday: August 26th, 2000

Doesn't Really Matter by Janet Jackson

"Doesn't Really Matter" was written and produced by Janet Jackson to be used as the theme for the Universal Pictures film Nutty Professor II: The Klumps. The lyrics are based on a poem Jackson wrote. My Birthday is August 26th, 2000 which is when this song was #1 on the song charts.
